About Sangola

Explore Sangola

Local Markets

The Sangola Sunday market, also known as Athavada Bazar, is a weekly market held in Sangola, Solapur, Maharashtra. It's a place where producers sell goods like grain, earthenware, yarn, and garden produce. While it's a gathering center, it also serves as a distribution point for various products.

Bus Stand

The Sangola bus stand, also known as a State Transport Bus Depot, is located on Bus Stand Road, Sangola Vita Road, near Kadlas Naka. It is a large and conveniently located bus stand with accessible facilities and is known for its punctuality and extensive network.

Babasaheb Ambedkar

Bhimrao Ramji Ambedkar, known as Babasaheb Ambedkar, was a prominent Indian jurist, economist, politician, and social reformer. He is best known as the chief architect of the Indian Constitution and the first Law Minister of independent India.

Education

Sangola Vidyamandir Jr College Sangola

Sangola Vidyamandir Junior College, located in Sangola, Maharashtra, is a well-established institution offering education from primary to junior college levels. It is managed by the Sangola Taluka Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, founded in 1952. The junior college (also known as an inter or intermediate college) focuses on preparing students for higher education or careers after secondary school.

Sangola Mahavidyalaya Sangola

Sangola Mahavidyalaya, located in Sangola, Maharashtra, is a college established in 1978 by the Sangola Taluka Uchcha Shikshan Mandal. It initially offered a single traditional course, B.A., with an initial enrollment of 50 students. Today, the college has grown to offer 5 undergraduate, 4 postgraduate programs, and houses two research centers in Hindi and Chemistry, with a student body of around 3000.

New English School Sangola

New English School in Sangola, established in 1969, is a private aided school managed by a non-governmental organization. It's located in the urban area of Sangola, Solapur district, Maharashtra, and caters to students from grades 5 to 12.

Railway Station

Sangola Railway Station, code SGLA, is a significant station in the Solapur district of Maharashtra, India. It is a part of the Indian Railway network and is known for being a popular station for train travel and ticket booking. Approximately 19 trains pass through this station.
